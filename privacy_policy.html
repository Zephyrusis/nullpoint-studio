Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026
Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Ascension Gym collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep things simple: most of your data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

Ascension Gym is operated by Nullpoint Studio ("we", "us", "our"). If you have questions about this policy, contact us at ascensiongymapp@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information stored only on your device:

  • Workout history, quest completions, and rep logs
  • Hunter profile (height, weight, max reps, difficulty preference)
  • Streak data, achievement progress, and personal records
  • App settings and notification preferences

This data is stored locally using Hive (an on-device database) and is never transmitted to our servers. It exists solely on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app or reset your progress.

Information collected by third-party services:

  • RevenueCat - processes subscription purchases and verifies entitlements. RevenueCat may collect your Google Play purchase token, a random user identifier, and transaction metadata. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing - handles payment processing. Google's privacy practices apply. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information directly. We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the Ascension Gym experience
  • To verify your subscription status via RevenueCat
  • To send local push notifications you have enabled in Settings
  • To store promo code redemptions so access persists across app restarts

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Promo Codes

If you redeem a promo code, we store the redemption date locally on your device to enforce the access window. No code or personal data associated with it is transmitted to us.

5. Push Notifications

Ascension Gym may send local push notifications (daily quest reminders, boss raid alerts, penalty countdowns). These are scheduled entirely on-device. No notification content is transmitted to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Ascension Gym is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All on-device data is deleted when you uninstall the app or use the "Reset All Progress" option in Settings. Subscription records held by RevenueCat are subject to their retention policy.

To request deletion of any data held by our third-party processors, contact RevenueCat directly or contact us and we will assist where possible.

8. Security

Because almost all data is stored only on your device, the security of that data depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at: ascensiongymapp@gmail.com
